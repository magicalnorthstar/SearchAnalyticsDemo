Create a Pinecone Account
Before you can use Pinecone, you need to create an account on Pinecone.io.
1.	Go to the Pinecone website: https://www.pinecone.io/.
2.	Sign up for a new account or log in if you already have one.
3.	Once you're logged in, you'll have access to the Pinecone dashboard, where you can manage your indexes and API keys.
